About YB Studio

Where artistry meets authenticity in every frame

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Yogesh Borekar - Photograpgy & Graphics Studio emerged from a simple belief: that every moment worth remembering deserves to be captured with both technical excellence and genuine emotion.

What began as a passion for photography has grown into a dedicated journey of capturing meaningful moments. Inspired by natural light and genuine human connections, I strive to bring this vision into every session I photograph.

Today, we're proud to have documented over 150 weddings, countless portraits, and numerous other events. Each project reinforces our commitment to creating images that transcend trends and stand the test of time.

Our Mission

To preserve life's most precious moments through artistry that honors authenticity, celebrates emotion, and creates timeless visual narratives that families will cherish for generations.

Our Values

The principles that guide every photograph we create.

Passion

Every photograph is crafted with genuine love for the art and our clients.

Excellence

We pursue perfection in every frame, combining technical skill with artistic vision.

Connection

Building authentic relationships allows us to capture genuine emotions.
